Magic is played in person with printed cards, or using a deck of virtual cards through the Internet-based Magic: The Gathering Online. To build a deck, you must have at least 60 cards, which consist of; creatures, lands, and instants or sorceries.

Because Magic the Gathering is played mostly in person, this allows people of many ages to get up and play a card game with others that share the same interests. There are also many activities relating to Magic the Gathering that take place besides friendly games, such as; tournaments and pre-release events.
